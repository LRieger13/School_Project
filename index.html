Final Project

Salutations and greetings from the new rip-roaring 20's. 100 years later and we've become quite obsessed with times long passed. The idea of using the 1920's as a theme for this site was just due to the times in which we live. I went with colors instead of the traditional black & white to give it a different feel to it and allow for more creativity. Being someone who has always had a heart that beat for jazz and a soul that craved all things speakeasy, modernizing this style was a fun personal project.
